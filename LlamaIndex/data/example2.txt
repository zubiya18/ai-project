1. Nike is a global leader in athletic footwear and apparel.
2. Founded in 1964 as Blue Ribbon Sports, it became Nike in 1971.
3. The iconic Nike Swoosh logo was designed by Carolyn Davidson in 1971.
4. Nike's mission is to "Bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete in the world."
5. Air Jordan, launched in 1984, revolutionized basketball sneaker culture.
6. Nike uses sustainable materials like recycled polyester in its products.
7. The brand sponsors top athletes like LeBron James and Serena Williams.
8. Nike's "Just Do It" slogan is one of the most recognizable in advertising.
9. The company invests heavily in R&D for performance-enhancing gear.
10. Nike's revenue surpassed $50 billion in 2023, dominating the sportswear market.
